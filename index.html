All About

Hello, my name is Elizabeth. I am an outgoing, confident women who has a wonderful family and loves to spend time with them, along with finding new ways to be creative within different aspects of my life such as crafting, designing and interior design.

I first got into crafts as a hobby on the side of my job and then decided to make a name for myself now known as Creative Crafts. Crafting lets me show my creative outlets. When I have free time, I focus it on crafting especially on the weekends, crafting to me is very therapeutic and is a period where I can have time to myself. I started Creative Crafts around 3 years ago. As a woman who works and does crafting on the side, I decided that a Facebook page would be the best way to show my creative outlets to friends and family. This worked for me as I was able to control when I could upload crafts and I was able to get others to share my page around. I feel that Facebook was the perfect starting point, as many people are on Facebook in general and on a daily basis, therefore, I would get the most out of Creative Crafts.

My Website

There are many crafts which are offered within my store such as stone pictures, family trees, sea glass pictures, seasonal wreaths, clay moulds, sketching and jewellery. The most popular of my crafts would be the stone pictures, family trees and sea glass pictures as these are all unique and all have different materials used.

I am able to customize my crafts to suit the individual buying the item and make it more personal towards them or as a gift. Personalised gifts sell very well as customers like to have a say in how the craft/crafts will turn out. In the past the most popular personalised gifts would be stone pictures for newly married couples, or stone/ glass pictures made for new-born babies. I also customise jewellery if the customer wants a certain shape, bead or colour and if it is for a child/adult.

What is a custom order?

The most popular requests are those made for custom orders, or those who would prefer a special gift made for a friend or loved one.
How this works? Fill in the Custom Order Form with your name, email, subject such as a card or frame with a personal message and click on Open Email. This opens your email platform on your computer or device so you can send us your email. We do not require any payment details here, only when you contact us and discuss what fantastic item you would like made. Or you can view our ebay listings to see if anything catches your eye.
We look forward to hearing from you.
